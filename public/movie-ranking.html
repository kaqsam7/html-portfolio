The Best Books According to Quashie-Sam

My Top Three (3) Books Of All Time.

The Lord of the Rings Trilogy

Epic Fantasy book that takes you on a journey with Frodo Baggins to destroy the one ring

Harry Potter

Follow orphan boy Harry Potter as he discovers what it means to be a wizard and the master of one's own destiny

The Chronicles of Narnia

Fantasy series following the Penvensie siblings and their adventures saving the land of Narnia from evil tyrants
